Making a Difference is Easy

Three simple steps to fight food waste.

📝

Post Surplus

Donors list their excess food with descriptions and a quick GPS-tagged landmark.

🔔

NGOs Notified

Verified NGOs view the active feed and claim items based on their community needs.

🚚

Direct Pickup

NGOs navigate to the donor's location and mark the items as picked up in real-time.

📋 Safety Guidelines

01

Quality First: Only donate food that you would eat yourself. Ensure items are fresh and within safe consumption dates.

02

Proper Packaging: Use clean, leak-proof, and food-grade containers. Label them clearly if they contain common allergens.

03

Clear Communication: Use the "Landmark" field to describe exactly where the NGO should park or find the package.
